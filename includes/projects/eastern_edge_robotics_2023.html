Eastern Edge Robotics 2023

Eastern Edge Robotics' 2023 remotely-operated vehicle (ROV), built for the MATE competition, is named Caribou.

Caribou features two Raspberry Pi compute modules in the enclosure along with a suite of devices including microcontrollers, ADCs, temperature sensors, electronic speed controllers, and DC motors.

As a software member, my contributions included:

  • Developing critical features, including the camera tab and the Pygame joystick listener
  • Bug fixes and adaptations to backend code
  • Being part of the competition deck crew
  • Flowcharts and documentation for competition deliverables
