Photosphere PCB

This project is a carrier printed circuit board (PCB) for the Raspberry Pi Compute Module 4 created in KiCad.

I designed the board at Eastern Edge Robotics to provide a dual MIPI CSI camera feed while meeting space constraints. It includes the following peripherals:

  • Two MIPI CSI camera ports
  • 5V Servo header
  • MicroUSB (USB 2.0) for data
  • USB-C for power
